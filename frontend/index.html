Equal Opportunity and Non-Discrimination at Bells Tech

Bells University of Technology values diversity and actively seeks talented students, faculty, and staff from varied backgrounds, experiences, and perspectives to drive innovation in mechatronics, robotics, and artificial intelligence (AI). The University is committed to providing a nurturing environment that fosters learning, creativity, and groundbreaking research in these fields. Bells Tech does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, sex, gender, religion, creed, national or ethnic origin, citizenship status, age, disability, veteran status, or any other legally protected class in its admissions, academic programs, or employment practices. This commitment extends to our laboratories, research initiatives, and all university-administered programs. For inquiries or concerns regarding this policy, contact the Office of Equal Opportunity and Academic Excellence, Bells University of Technology, KM 8, Idiroko Road, Ota, Ogun State, Nigeria; or call +234 (0)809-123-4567.
